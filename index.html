The pistachio cake like no other

This recipe is completely original, passed down from my grandma soon to become my mom's favorite, for good reason. It's tasty, creamy and just overall perfect, from the texture to the taste. I've decided to share it with you, and trust me, this unique pistachio cake is worth trying!

A simple sponge cake layer and a coacoa layer, with pistachio cream in between as well as on top, not only is it aesthetically pleasing to the eye, but incredibly delicious packed with deep pistachio flavour!


Here's what you'll essentially need:

Layer 1
  • 3 eggs
  • 6tbsp sugar
  • 3tbsp water
  • 6tbsp flour
  • 5g baking powder

Layer 2
  • 6 egg whites
  • 120g sugar
  • 1tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1tbsp cocoa
  • 120g grounded walnuts
  • 5g baking powder
  • 3tbsp flour

Cream
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 egg
  • 150g sugar
  • 60ml milk
  • 250g butter
  • 2tsp pistachio extract

Preparation step by step:

Layer 1

Mix 3 eggs with 6tbsp of sugar for 10 minutes, then add 3tbsp water, 6tbsp flour and the baking powder. Bake in the oven at 170Â°C until it passes the toothpick test.

Layer 2

Mix 6 egg whites with 120g sugar until you get a white foam. Add 1tbsp sunflower oil, 1tbsp cocoa, 120g grounded walnuts, 5g baking powder and 3tbsp flour. Mix again and bake.

Cream

Mix 6 egg yolks, 1egg, 150g sugar and 60ml milk. Mix again at low fire until you get a cream. Let it cool for about 30mins then add the butter and pistachio extract.

Assemblage

Smear the second layer with cream, add the first layer on top, smear with cream again and optionally you can sprinkle some walnut, chocolate or topping of your liking on.

Let it cool in the fridge and dig in!


All done! Are you ready to give it a taste with your friends and family? You're sure to get compliments for this irresistible, delightful cake.

Bon Appetit!
